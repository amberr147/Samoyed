What is a Samoyed?

The Samoyed is a fluffy, friendly, and intelligent dog breed known for its **"Sammy smile"**.

Characteristics

  • Medium to large-sized with a thick white coat
  • Friendly and social, great with families
  • Energetic and playful, loves outdoor activities
  • Originally bred to herd reindeer and pull sleds
